# Privacy Policy

**Last Updated:** December 29, 2025

## Introduction

Pokemon Master Set Tracker ("we," "us," "our," or "the App") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mobile application.

Please read this privacy policy carefully. If you do not agree with our policies and practices, please do not use our App.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### 1.1 Information You Provide Directly

- **User Account Information**: When creating a user account, we collect your username and any profile information you provide.
- **Collection Data**: Information about your Pokemon card collections, including card details, quantities, and condition notes.
- **Favorites**: Cards you mark as favorites or save within the application.
- **Search and Filter Preferences**: Your search queries and filter settings to improve app functionality.

### 1.2 Information Collected Automatically

- **Device Information**: Device model, operating system version, unique device identifiers, and mobile network information.
- **Usage Data**: Information about how you interact with the App, including features accessed, time spent, and actions taken.
- **Crash Reports**: Automatic crash data to help us fix bugs and improve stability.
- **Analytics**: General usage statistics and performance metrics.

### 1.3 Third-Party Data Sources

- **Pokemon TCG API**: We retrieve public Pokemon trading card data from the Pokemon Trading Card Game API to provide card information and pricing data.
- **TCGPlayer Pricing**: We may fetch current market pricing information from TCGPlayer to display card values.

---

## 2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect to:

- **Provide and Improve the App**: Deliver core functionality, personalization, and enhance user experience
- **Manage Your Account**: Create, maintain, and manage your user account and collections
- **Analytics**: Understand app usage patterns and user preferences
- **Bug Fixes**: Diagnose technical issues and improve app stability
- **Customer Support**: Respond to your inquiries and provide technical support
- **Security**: Protect against fraud, unauthorized access, and other security threats
- **Legal Compliance**: Comply with applicable laws and regulations

---

## 3. How We Share Your Information

### 3.1 Information We Do NOT Share

- We **do not sell** your personal information to third parties
- We **do not share** your collection data with other users without your explicit consent
- We **do not share** login credentials or sensitive account information

### 3.2 Limited Sharing Scenarios

We may share information only:

- **With Service Providers**: Third-party services that assist us in operating the App (analytics, crash reporting, hosting)
- **For Legal Reasons**: When required by law, regulation, or valid legal process
- **With Your Consent**: When you explicitly authorize sharing with other users or third parties
- **Public APIs**: Public card data from the Pokemon TCG API is used only for display purposes

---

## 4. Data Storage and Security

### 4.1 Local Storage

- Most of your data (collections, favorites, preferences) is stored **locally on your device** in a secure SQLite database
- This data is encrypted and not automatically synced to external servers

### 4.2 Cloud Storage

- User account information is stored securely using industry-standard encryption
- We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your data
- We use HTTPS for all network communications

### 4.3 Data Retention

- Collection and account data is retained as long as your account is active
- You can request deletion of your account and associated data at any time
- Deleted data is removed within 30 days
